    Nairobi Property Market Overview

    For the past two decades, the Kenyan real estate market has grown exponentially as evidenced by its contribution to the country’s GDP which grew from 10.5% in 2000 to 12.6% in 2012 and 13.8% in 2016. The growth is driven by mostly Infrastructural developments such as improved roads, utility connections, upgrade of key airports and stable GDP growth which has averaged at 5.4% over the last 5 years against a Sub- Saharan average of 4.1%;
